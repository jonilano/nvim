*scrooloose/nerdtree*

   |,N|   open tree

   |o|    open
   |i|    open in split
   |t|    open in tab
   |T|    open in tab, silently

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*splitjoin*
Multiple lines into single lines (and vice versa).

   |gS|  expand to multiple lines
   |gJ|  collapse

Example:
>
    <div>xxx</div>

    <div>
      xxx
    </div>

   |cs"'|         "hello" --> 'hello'
   |cs'<q>|       'hello' --> <q>hello</q>
